Redefining Career Re-Entry for Women in Botswana

We empower women to successfully return to the workforce and help employers access a vibrant, diverse talent pool.

The Challenges We Face

Skills Gap

Women returning after a career break often find their skills are no longer up-to-date with current industry standards.

Lack of access to professional development and training opportunities can make it difficult to re-enter the workforce.

Bias & Stereotypes

Recruitment processes often hold unconscious biases against candidates with career gaps on their resumes.

These biases can lead to women being overlooked for roles they are perfectly qualified for, based on outdated perceptions.

Lack of Support

Many women lack the necessary support systems, such as mentorship and networking, to navigate their career return.

This isolation can hinder their job search and make it difficult to reintegrate into a professional environment.

Our Solutions

Skills Re-Entry Workshops

Our workshops are designed to bridge the gap between a woman's previous skills and the current demands of the job market. We focus on in-demand technical and soft skills, ensuring our participants are ready to compete effectively.

We cover everything from modern digital tools and data literacy to leadership and communication skills, providing a holistic and empowering learning experience.

Mentorship & Networking

We connect women with experienced professionals in their target industries. These mentorship relationships provide invaluable guidance, support, and a personal connection to navigate the challenges of re-entry.

Our networking events help build a strong community of like-minded individuals, creating a support system that lasts long after the program ends.

Employer Partnership Program

We work directly with companies to create return-to-work programs that value the skills and experiences of women with career breaks. We help our partners implement inclusive hiring practices and access our pre-vetted talent pool.

This program benefits employers by broadening their talent pipeline and enriching their workplace with diverse perspectives and experiences.

Our Impact

EmpowerHer Pathways creates a ripple effect, positively impacting individuals, businesses, and society as a whole.

  • For Women:

    Increased confidence, faster job search, higher success rates.

  • For Employers:

    Access to diverse talent, enhanced DEI, higher retention.

  • For Society:

    Closes gender pay gap, boosts economic empowerment.
