The Problem: The Systemic Gap Penalty

The Talent Lockout

Highly skilled and educated women returning to the workforce after a career break (typically for caregiving) face systemic barriers, causing a Systemic Gap Penalty that prevents re-entry at their pre-break career level.

  • 73% of women who take a career break have trouble finding a job upon return.
  • Skilled returners face a **permanent earnings penalty** and reduced career satisfaction.

The Corporate Pain

Hiring Companies struggle with talent shortages, especially for senior roles, and often resort to costly, risky ex-pat talent due to a lack of verifiable, local talent pipelines.

The Solution: End-to-End Talent Validation

1. AI Talent Vault & Tracking

We track the professional's pre-break information and manage their profile during the career break, allowing for **immediate onboarding after graduation** or break.

2. Psychometric Profiling & Skills Matching

AI defines current skills, provides **psychometric tests for best placement**, and matches them to personalized career paths.

3. Verification: Audited Capstone

Users complete a real-world project, audited by senior professionals, providing **verifiable proof of skill**.

4. Placement & Development

We actively place certified talent, turning the D&I mandate into a high-value talent solution.
