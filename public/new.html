Our Core Mission

To empower women who have taken career breaks—for family, health, or personal reasons—by equipping them with modern skills and connecting them to inclusive employers.

Skill Modernization

Stay competitive with courses in AI, Cloud Computing, and Digital Marketing.

Mentorship & Coaching

Personalized guidance from industry veterans who understand the return journey.

Direct Job Placement

Exclusive access to roles at companies committed to diversity and inclusion.

Our Specialized Pathways

Tech Refresh Program

6-Month Accelerated Course

Focuses on quickly updating skills in high-demand tech areas like Data Analysis and modern Software Development stacks. Includes a capstone project.

  • Cloud Fundamentals (AWS/Azure)
  • Python for Data Science
  • Agile Methodology Training

Leadership Reclaim Track

3-Month Executive Coaching

Designed for women returning to senior or managerial roles. Focuses on confidence building, negotiation, and strategic thinking.

  • Advanced Communication Skills
  • Conflict Resolution Workshops
  • Executive Presence Training

Entrepreneurial Spark

1-Year Startup Incubator

Supports women starting their own ventures. Provides funding access, legal support, and hands-on business modeling.

  • Pitch Deck Development
  • Seed Funding Connections
  • Legal and IP Guidance
