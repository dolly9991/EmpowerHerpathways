The Problem: High Risk, Low Visibility

For Corporations: High Risk & Non-Compliance

  • Recruitment Gamble: Hundreds of hours spent filtering CVs that lack verifiable skill data.
  • Compliance Pressure: Increasing regulatory and financial penalties for failing to meet localization (HRDC) and diversity targets.
  • Talent Drain: Forced reliance on expensive expatriate talent due to lack of trust in the local pipeline.

For Skilled Women: The Access Gap

  • Systemic Gap Penalty: 73% Highly skilled women returning after a career break face massive barriers to re-entry at their previous seniority level.
  • Uncertainty: Certifications and training programs are often not standardized or trusted by enterprise HR systems.
  • Economic Inequality: This friction costs the economy millions and prevents equitable growth.

This is not a skills gap; it's a verification and trust gap that costs both sides dearly.

The Solution: Verified Placements (Our Moat)

We are a talent de-risking engine built on a proprietary, three-stage vetting platform.

1. Skill Audit & Matching

AI defines current skills and matches them to high-demand career paths using market data.

2. Verification: Audited Capstone

Users complete real-world projects, which are audited by a network of senior professionals, providing verifiable proof of skill.

3. Verified Placement Profile

The client receives a tamper-proof digital record—a guaranteed profile that meets specific technical and compliance requirements.

The result: Clients hire with a zero-risk guarantee that transforms D&I compliance from a liability into a high-value talent acquisition strategy.
