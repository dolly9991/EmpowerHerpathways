Relaunch Your Career with Confidence

EmpowerHer Pathways helps Botswana women return to the workforce after career breaks through mentorship, skill-building, and networking opportunities.

Our Mission

Empowering Women Returning to Work

EmpowerHer Pathways is dedicated to empowering women to overcome the unique challenges of re-entering the workforce. We provide tailored resources, a supportive community, and practical tools to help them rediscover their potential and secure fulfilling careers.

The Challenges We Face

Skills Gap

Women returning after a career break often find their skills are no longer up-to-date with current industry standards.

Lack of access to professional development and training opportunities can make it difficult to re-enter the workforce.

Bias & Stereotypes

Recruitment processes often hold unconscious biases against candidates with career gaps on their resumes.

These biases can lead to women being overlooked for roles they are perfectly qualified for, based on outdated perceptions.

Lack of Support

Many women lack the necessary support systems, such as mentorship and networking, to navigate their career return.

This isolation can hinder their job search and make it difficult to reintegrate into a professional environment.

Our Impact

EmpowerHer Pathways creates a ripple effect, positively impacting individuals, businesses, and society as a whole.

  • For Women:

    Increased confidence, faster job search, higher success rates.

  • For Employers:

    Access to diverse talent, enhanced DEI, higher retention.

  • For Society:

    Closes gender pay gap, boosts economic empowerment.
